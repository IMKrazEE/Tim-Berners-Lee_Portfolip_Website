Hi User!

Tim Berners-Lee is a software engineer who invented the internet in 1989 while at SCERN, the European Particle Space Physics Laboratory. He is the director of WWW or World Wide Web consortium that he founded and a professor in the computer science department at the university of oxford. In 2001, he became a fellow of royal society and in 2004, he was knighted.

In 1994, he founded the World Wide Web at the laboratory of computer Science at the Massathusetts Institute of Technology in Boston. It was made up of companies that wanted to work together to create web standards and also improve the web. It was quickly aggreed that the technology should be free of royalties and patents. Berners-Lee has served as a director of this W3C since it was founded. In 1995 Microsoft produced the first version of Internet Explorer and this commercial browser created enormoros growth in website and webusers. The web was first used for E-commerce in 1997.
